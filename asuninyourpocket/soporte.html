Frequently Asked Questions

A Sun in Your Pocket turns your iPhone into a personal planetarium. It offers real-time visualizations of the sun and moon, elegant widgets, and precise predictions of astronomical events like sunrises, sunsets, moon phases, solstices, and equinoxes.

All calculations are performed locally on your device based on your location. This means they work offline and your location data never leaves your device.

A Sun in Your Pocket requires iOS 17.0 or later.

Troubleshooting

App closes unexpectedly

1. Update to the latest version from the App Store
2. Restart your iPhone/iPad
3. If persists, contact support@mirloapps.es stating your device model and iOS version

Location data is inaccurate

1. Verify that "Location Services" are enabled
2. Go to Settings → Privacy → Location Services
3. Allow "While Using App" access for A Sun in Your Pocket

I don't see my widgets

1. Some widgets require the Premium version
2. Restart your iPhone/iPad
3. Remove and re-add the widget

Weather forecast not loading

1. Check your internet connection
2. Ensure you have the Premium version active
3. Restart the app and try again
