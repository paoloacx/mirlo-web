Troubleshooting

App closes unexpectedly

1. Update to the latest version from the App Store
2. Restart your device
3. If persists, contact us stating your device model and iOS version

Issues with iCloud

1. Verify your iCloud connection in iOS Settings
2. Ensure you have available space
3. Check that sync is enabled in the app
