Are you truly unique? Daily psychological analysis powered by AI. Find your Tribe, compare global stats, and visualize your unique personality fingerprint. Do you really know who you are? In a world full of noise, finding clarity about your own identity is the ultimate challenge. Who Am I is not just a personality test—it is a daily journey of introspection, a psychological mirror, and a gateway to understanding your place in the world. Through a minimalist and calming interface, answering just one deep question a day allows our advanced AI to construct a living, breathing map of your psyche. KEY FEATURES Daily Introspection Receive one carefully curated psychological question every day. These are not random queries; they are calibrated to measure core dimensions of your personality: Introversion, Reflection, Tradition, and Planning. Over time, these answers weave a complex tapestry of your true self. The Nexus: Compare With The World Connect anonymously to The Nexus, our global consciousness database. Find Your Tribe: Are you a mystical "Soul", a pragmatic "Geyser", or a burning "Flame"? See where you belong among thousands of unique profiles. Global Statistics: Discover if your opinions make you a conformist or a divergent. See real-time percentages of how the rest of the world answered the same tough questions. Rankings: Track your introspection streak and see how your commitment compares to the global community. AI-Powered Analysis Your personal Oracle analyzes your daily inputs to provide profound insights. Evolution Tracking: Watch how your personality shifts over weeks and months. You are not static; see your growth visualize before your eyes. Dynamic Archetypes: You are more than a label. As your answers change, your reflected Archetype evolves with you. Stunning Visualizations Uniqueness Glyphs: Every user generates a unique, generative 6x6 glyph based on their choices. No two are exactly alike. Branching Timeline: Visualize your life's path as an infinite, growing tree where every decision creates a new branch. Premium Aesthetics: A quiet, ad-free environment designed for focus and contemplation, featuring dynamic backgrounds and fluid animations. Completely Private Your journey is yours alone. All data is stored securely. When you compare with The Nexus, your identity remains completely anonymous. We believe that true vulnerability requires absolute safety. Why download Who Am I? Most apps demand your attention. This app demands your intention. Spend 30 seconds a day to gain a lifetime of self-awareness.
