Troubleshooting

App closes unexpectedly

1. Update to the latest version from the App Store
2. Restart your device
3. If it persists, contact us stating your device model and iOS version

iCloud Issues

1. Check your iCloud connection in iOS Settings
2. Ensure you have available space
3. Check that synchronization is enabled in device settings
