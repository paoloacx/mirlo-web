Troubleshooting

App closes unexpectedly

1. Update to the latest version of yeah! from the App Store
2. Restart your iPhone/iPad
3. If it persists, contact support@mirloapps.es stating your iPhone model and iOS version

App doesn't sync with iCloud

1. Verify you are signed into iCloud in iOS Settings
2. Ensure you have available space in iCloud
3. Check that sync is enabled in yeah! → Settings

I don't see my widgets

1. Ensure you have the Premium version active
2. Restart your iPhone/iPad
3. Remove and re-add the widget

Location is not accurate

Ensure location services are enabled (Settings → Privacy → Location Services → yeah!), be outdoors if possible, and allow "While Using App" access.
